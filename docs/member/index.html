Teachers

Feixiang Du

Senior Lecturer

Feixiang Du is faculty at School of Electrical Engineering, Tongling University. Feixiang Du obtained the M.S. degree from Taiyuan University of Technology in 2018. Currently, Feixiang Du is leading the IntelliPupil Microvision Artificial Intelligence Laboratory and exploring how to understand and interact with the world via the computer vision. His research interests include Object Detection, Image Segmentation and Hyperspectral Reconstruction.
